                                                                                                        Employee Training Score Analysis:


1) In this project,imported the json file of employee which has been provided.
2) After importing json file,using mongo shell entered into database of employee and also into the collection of employee data.
3) Then listing the employees who has failed in term1 i.e employee who score less than 37 and also finding their count.
4) Finding the average of term1 score using "aggregate" function.
5) Then next executed the average of employees who passed in all 3 terms using "and" operator.
6) And finally executed the average of individual employee of all 3 terms using "aggregate" and "$project" command .
